Research Intelligence Platform

Upload, analyse, and connect academic papers with AI. Surface insights, identify gaps, and collaborate with your team — all in one workspace.

Features
⊞
Paper Library
Upload PDFs — AI extracts summaries, reliability assessments, and research topics automatically.
◻
Research Chat
Ask questions across your entire library. The AI cites exact passages from your uploaded papers.
⚡
Collision Engine
Automatically detects when two papers contain contradictory claims — surfaces conflicts instantly.
📊
Contribution Heatmap
GitHub-style activity grid showing each collaborator's uploads, analyses, and annotations over time.
🧩
Concept Deconstructor
Break dense equations and complex ideas into scaffolded learning modules for different expertise levels.
◈
Knowledge Graph
Interactive visual map of how papers connect. Drag, zoom, and explore topic clusters.
Quick Start

4
Detect contradictions Visit Collaboration tab to see the Semantic Collision Engine.
5
Use the Deconstructor Paste any dense equation to get scaffolded explanations.
