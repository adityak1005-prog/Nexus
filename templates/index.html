Research Intelligence Platform

Upload, analyse, and connect academic papers with AI. Surface insights, identify gaps, and collaborate with your team — all in one workspace.

Features
⊞
Paper Library
Upload PDF papers and let AI extract summaries, reliability assessments, and research topics automatically.
◻
Research Chat
Ask questions across your entire library. The AI cites exact passages from your uploaded papers.
◎
Gap Analysis
Identify contradictions, unexplored areas, and research gaps across all papers in your project.
◈
Knowledge Graph
Visualise how papers connect through shared topics. Interactive nodes — drag, zoom, and explore.
◇
Diagram Generator
Convert any paper into flowcharts, mind maps, timelines, or concept maps — fully scrollable and exportable.
▦
Team Collaboration
Invite collaborators, add shared annotations, and track who uploaded and analysed each paper.
Quick Start Guide
1
Create a project Use the sidebar selector or the button above. Give it a name and a research problem statement.
2
Upload PDF papers Drag & drop into the Library or use the ↑ Upload button. Files are parsed and embedded automatically.
3
Analyse each paper Open a paper and click Analyse. AI generates a summary, reliability score, and research topics.
4
Chat with your library Ask questions — "What methods were used?" or "Which papers contradict each other?" — and get cited answers.
5
Explore insights & visuals Run gap analysis, view the knowledge graph, or generate diagrams from any paper.
